About Kent Ridge Hall CCA Points Counter

Kent Ridge Hall CCA Points Counter belongs to the KR Utility series, which aims to ease your life in KR.

This points counter's logic is based on the following assumptions and conditions:

  • This follows, to its best understanding, the points briefing AY24/25 PowerPoint slide.
  • For sports CCAs, all roles except Members are assumed to be in the training team and earn an extra 6 points.
  • When the custom point input is not available, it is assumed that you get the highest possible points for that CCA and role.

Please note the following when using the points counter:

  • After selecting you choices and calculating, the selection will be disabled, and the results will appear. Reload the page to perform another calculation.
